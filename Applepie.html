Apple pie

What is an Apple pie

An apple pie is a pie or a tart, in which the principal filling ingredient is apple .
It is, on occasion, served with whipped cream or ice cream on top, or alongside cheddar cheese.
The pastry is generally used top-and-bottom, making it a double-crust pie; the upper crust may be a circular or a pastry lattice woven of crosswise strips.
Depending on the baker's preference, the bottom of the double-crust may be baked first (before baking the whole pie) to prevent the bottom from getting soggy.
Exceptions are deep-dish apple pie , with a top crust only, and open-face Tarte Tatin.
The apple pie is one of the United State's signature comfort foods.
